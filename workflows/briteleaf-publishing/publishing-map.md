# Publishing Map

## Stage 1 — Topic Selection
Choose a topic with:
- search value
- audience relevance
- strong curiosity or confusion factor
- monetization potential
- repurposing potential

## Stage 2 — Article Build
Create the main article first:
- clear title
- strong hook
- practical breakdown
- direct verdict
- CTA

## Stage 3 — Asset Extraction
Pull from the article:
- best hooks
- strongest claims
- biggest risk points
- best short explanations
- strongest CTA angle

## Stage 4 — Platform Adaptation
Turn the article into:
- short-form video scripts
- social captions
- Pinterest content
- follow-up post ideas

## Stage 5 — Publishing Order
1. publish article
2. create linked support content
3. distribute discovery assets
4. monitor and refine

## Stage 6 — Review
Check:
- clarity
- consistency
- SEO alignment
- cross-platform usefulness
- quality of repurposing
