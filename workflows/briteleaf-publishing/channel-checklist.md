# Channel Checklist

## Website / Article
- title is clear
- intro is strong
- structure is readable
- verdict is direct
- CTA is present

## Short-Form Video
- hook is strong in first line
- message is concise
- takeaway is clear
- CTA fits platform

## Pinterest
- title is search-friendly
- description is clear
- visual angle is obvious
- article link is aligned

## Social Caption
- opening line is strong
- text is easy to scan
- message supports article
- CTA is simple and clear
